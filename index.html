    Rajshahi College

    Founded in 1873, Rajshahi College is the third oldest institution of higher education in Bangladesh, after Dhaka College and Chittagong College. Centrally located in Rajshahi city beside Rajshahi Collegiate School and close to the Varendra Research Museum, it has been affiliated with the National University of Bangladesh and continues to serve tens of thousands of students each year.
